About ACEP

The Academic Center for Editing and Publishing (ACEP) is dedicated to elevating the quality and impact of academic work across various disciplines. We understand the rigorous demands of scholarly communication and are committed to providing unparalleled support to our diverse clientele.

Our team comprises experienced editors, proofreaders, and publishing specialists with deep expertise in academic writing conventions, citation styles, and journal requirements. We partner with students, researchers, faculty, journals, and publishers to ensure their work is clear, concise, coherent, and meets the highest academic standards.

At ACEP, we believe that well-edited and professionally presented research is crucial for effective knowledge dissemination and career advancement. Let us be your trusted partner in achieving academic excellence.

Executive Manager

Prof. Mohamed El-Sherif, Ph.D.

Prof. Mohamed El-Sherif is a distinguished Full Professor at the Department of Surgery, Faculty of Veterinary Medicine, New Valley University. With a profound academic background and extensive experience in scholarly communication, he brings a unique blend of practical expertise and academic rigor to ACEP.

Holding a Ph.D. in Veterinary Surgery from Damanhour University, Prof. El-Sherif has dedicated years to teaching Animal Surgery, Anesthesia, and Diagnostic Imaging Techniques to both undergraduate and postgraduate students. His career spans various esteemed academic and research roles, including Associate and Assistant Professor positions, and a significant tenure as a researcher and veterinarian.

Beyond his teaching and research, Prof. El-Sherif's commitment to academic excellence is further demonstrated by his role as a Managing Editor for the New Valley Veterinary Journal (NVVJ) and his previous chairmanship of the Research Ethics Committee at New Valley University. His deep understanding of research integrity, publication standards, and the nuances of academic writing ensures that ACEP is led by an individual truly dedicated to empowering research excellence.
